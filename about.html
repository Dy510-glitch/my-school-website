Our Origins

Founded in 1995, Barangay Tibay College started as a small learning center dedicated to providing quality education to the local community.

Mission & Vision

Mission: To empower students with the skills needed for the modern world.

Vision: To be the leading institution of innovation and integrity in the region.

Our Founder

The college was established by Dr. Juan Tibay, a visionary educator who believed that "Strength comes from Knowledge."
